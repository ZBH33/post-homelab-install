# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our standards
All contributors and maintainers are expected to treat others with respect, be considerate, and act professionally.

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers at `zbillman.work@gmail.com`.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers or, if applicable, via the organization's security contact. Reports will be handled in a timely and confidential manner.
